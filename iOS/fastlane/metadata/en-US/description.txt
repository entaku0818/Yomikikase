Voice Narrator is the all-in-one text-to-speech (TTS) app that reads anything aloud — PDFs, web pages, EPUB books, Google Drive files, scanned documents, and more. No account needed. Everything stays on your device.

7 WAYS TO ADD CONTENT
- Text input: type or paste any text and listen instantly
- PDF: import documents, books, and reports
- Web pages: paste a URL — any article read aloud
- EPUB: listen to ebooks from your library
- Google Drive: stream files directly from cloud storage
- Scanner: point your camera at printed text and listen
- TXT files: import plain text from any source

REAL-TIME TEXT HIGHLIGHTING
See every word highlighted as it's read aloud. Auto-scroll keeps you in sync with the audio — great for studying, following along, or checking pronunciation. Works in PDFs too.

DESIGNED FOR ACCESSIBILITY
Built for users with dyslexia, visual impairments, or reading fatigue. Adjustable speed, pitch, and multiple voice options help everyone find their ideal listening experience.

PLAYBACK CONTROLS
- Reading speed: 0.5x to 2x
- Voice pitch adjustment
- Background playback — keep listening while using other apps

PERSONALIZATION
- Custom dictionary: correct mispronounced names, technical terms, and brand names
- 10+ languages supported
- Save frequently used texts for quick replay

WHO IT'S FOR
- Students reviewing notes or textbooks hands-free
- Professionals catching up on documents during commutes
- Language learners improving listening comprehension
- Anyone with dyslexia, low vision, or reading fatigue

PREMIUM
- Remove all ads
- Unlimited PDF storage (free plan: 3 files)

Privacy: No account required. All content is processed on-device.
