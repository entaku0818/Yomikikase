Turn any text into speech — PDFs, websites, EPUB books, Google Drive files, scanned documents, and more. Voice Narrator is the all-in-one text-to-speech app that fits into your daily life.

KEY FEATURES

Read Any Content Aloud
- PDF files: import and listen to documents, reports, and books
- Web pages: paste a URL and hear any article read to you
- EPUB: listen to ebooks from your library
- Google Drive: open files directly from your cloud storage
- Document Scanner: point your camera at any printed text and listen instantly
- TXT files: import plain text from any source

Powerful Playback Controls
- Adjust reading speed from 0.5x to 2x (or faster)
- Control pitch to find the most comfortable voice for you
- Background playback — keep listening while using other apps
- Mini player for quick access during multitasking

Personalization
- Custom user dictionary: fix mispronounced names, technical terms, or brand names
- Multiple language support: switch between languages for multilingual content
- Save frequently used texts for instant replay

Accessibility First
- Designed for users with dyslexia, visual impairments, or reading fatigue
- No account required — just open and listen
- All processing done on-device for privacy

WHO IT IS FOR
- Students who want to review notes or textbooks hands-free
- Professionals who listen to reports and documents during commutes
- Language learners who improve listening comprehension
- Anyone who prefers listening over reading

Privacy: No account required. Your content stays on your device.
