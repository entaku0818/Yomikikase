Voice Your Text is a TTS app that reads aloud any text, PDF, or file. Perfect for multitasking — listen hands-free during commutes, workouts, or household chores.

【Key Features】

🎧 Background Playback
Playback continues even when you close the screen. Control reading from the notification panel — play, pause, or skip without opening the app.

📖 Text to Speech
Type or paste any text and hear it read aloud instantly.

📄 PDF Reader
Open PDF files directly and listen to them. Great for long documents and articles.

📂 Text File Import
Load .txt files and read them aloud with a single tap.

🔗 Share from Any App
Share text from your browser, notes, or any app — Voice Your Text reads it immediately.

🌍 7 Languages
Japanese, English, Chinese, Korean, French, German, and Spanish.

🎛 Speed & Pitch Control
Adjust reading speed from 0.5x to 2.0x, and fine-tune the voice pitch.

📋 History
Your last 50 read texts are saved. Tap any entry to replay it.

🏠 Home Screen Widget
Launch the app instantly from your home screen.

【Perfect for】
· Listening to articles and documents during your commute
· Enjoying books and content while cooking or exercising
· Language learning and pronunciation practice
· Resting your eyes while consuming content
· Reviewing work documents hands-free
